Elect Amber Collins-Gebrehiwet

My name is er Collins-Gebrehiwet. I am running for Judge in Marion County Circuit Court. Throughout my legal career, I have been committed to serving the Indianapolis community. I currently serve as the Magistrate in Circuit Court. Prior to taking the bench, I served as a Deputy Prosecutor in Marion County for over seven years. I am dedicated to helping the community both on and off the bench, as evidenced by my legacy of volunteerism. Because I have worked in Circuit Court and am familiar with the cases, I will be able to keep the Circuit Court running efficiently if I am elected Judge. My goal is to serve the litigants and attorneys who come before the Court with fairness, compassion, and adherence to the law. If I have the honor of being elected, Circuit Court will be known for civility and professionalism.

Fairness. Compassion. Civility.
